THE ARCHITECT'S VISION

25 Years Engineering Data Resiliency.

Hari Jandhyala is a Senior Data Architect and Migration Architect with deep expertise in enterprise data platforms, MDM, and legacy modernization. Currently leading the multi-year migration of SAG-AFTRA's pension-benefit platform — seven decades of participant data — from Oracle/mainframe to a MySQL microservices architecture.

His career spans the full data-warehouse lifecycle: from 8TB+ builds at Pfizer and Boeing to MDM implementations across Pfizer, VF Corp, and GSK, to Oracle Cloud SCM product ownership at Oracle Corp. He bridges executive strategy with hands-on architecture.

Multi-cloud certified (AWS Solutions Architect – Professional, Azure AZ-305, TOGAF 9.1, Oracle Certified Professional) with a track record of delivering complex programs in roughly half the expected timeframe.
